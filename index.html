hi.

welcome to the nicest place on the internet.

Having one of those days? Yeah, been there too. And sometimes, a little pick-me-up is hard to come by. So come on by to turn the sad into happy and the happy into a celebration. Cause this is a nice place to visit on days like today.

Like the song? It's "I Have Never Loved Someone" by My Brightest Diamond. We used the audio from La Blogotheque's Take Away Show found here. Created by Jeff & Lauren. Developed by Kenny.

TheNicestPlace.net is a reboot of TheNicestPlaceOnTheInter.net, a website which has brought me so much peace and comfort in times of need. The site has been offline for a few years now, but in light of current events and widespread self-isolation, I felt it valuable to restore this website, replicated from its latest source code from archive.org, and updated slightly for modern web browsers. I take no credit for this piece of living art, but I am glad to be able to maintain it for all to enjoy.
- Peace and love ♥ Toby

bring it in.

three easy steps

step one.

Record a hug and share the love. Turn on your webcam or any camera and just give it a good squeeze. We like people, animals, zombies, astronauts... really anything.

step two.

Upload your hug to YouTube and email me a link so I can add it to the YouTube playlist. Or send us a download link from a site like WeTransfer or YouSendIt and we can add it that way too.

step three.

In the email, tell us about yourself. Your name and your location so we know where in the world you're reppin'.

2020: New submissions can now be added to a playlist containing all the original videos.
